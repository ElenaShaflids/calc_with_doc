Simple Console Calculator¶

add(x, y)¶

simple_calculator.add(x, y)¶

Adds two numbers.

Parameters:

  • x -- First number.

  • y -- Second number.

Returns:

Sum of numbers x and y.

subtract(x, y)¶

simple_calculator.subtract(x, y)¶

Subtracts one number from another.

Parameters:

  • x -- First number.

  • y -- Second number.

Returns:

Difference of numbers x and y.

simple_calculator.multiply(x, y)¶

Multiplies two numbers.

Parameters:

  • x -- First number.

  • y -- Second number.

Returns:

Product of numbers x and y.

divide(x, y)¶

simple_calculator.divide(x, y)¶

Divides one number by another.

Parameters:

  • x -- Dividend.

  • y -- Divisor.

Returns:

The result of dividing x by y, or an error message when dividing by zero.
